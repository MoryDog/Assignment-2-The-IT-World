Hứa Nam Huy

Over the course of the last few weeks that I’ve been working with my teammates, I can say that everyone so far has been very hardworking and responsible towards the group’s projects. Although I have worked with many teams before due to the requirements of previous courses, I find it to be a new experience everytime I group up with new students since you’re basically working with people that you don’t know a lot about. In order for the team to work effectively, we often held meetings and also divided the assignment sections into smaller parts with each person in charge of a particular part so we can better manage time and keep track of the deadline. Quan – our team coordinator is resposible for organizing all of our meetings and even arranged the interview with an IT expert, he is a resourceful, responsible guy who always finished his tasks before the deadlines. While I didn’t talk much with Tri during meetings, he was a friendly and professionial person as his work was always detailed and carefully researched and he always try to catch up on what he missed. Duy didn’t show up to a lot of meetings because he has his own reasons but we have worked together on designing the website for our group report and I can say that he has been very skillful and was very active in finding and reporting on important information for contributing to our project idea. For myself, I think I’ve done well at managing the team’s progress and finding referencing materials for our team along with trying to code a website using my limited experience in web design. Overall, my teammates have all gave their best when doing the assignments, they were all communicative, responsible and I was very comfortable when working with everyone in the team.
